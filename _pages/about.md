I am a first year PhD student in the Astronomy and Astrophysics department at the University of Chicago, currently focused on employing computational and statistical techniques on both simulated and observational data for applications in cosmology and astrophysics. Currently, I use this site primarily as a repository for linking my [publications](https://dhayaaanbajagane.github.io/publications/) to the analysis code we employed in each work, and more importantly, the resulting data products we produced during analysis.

Research Interests
------

* Mass-estimation of Galaxy Clusters for cosmology
	* Understanding the origin of the scatter in a given scaling relation
* Convergence tests of multiple hydrodynamics simulations
* N-pt correlation functions
* *Interpretable* Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence for astrophysics/cosmology
	* Eg. Using Tree Regressors and CNNs but with complementary methods like [SHAP](https://github.com/slundberg/shap))
